03 / How It Works

From order to invoice
in four steps.

PurchasePlus handles the full procurement cycle so your team can focus on hospitality, not paperwork.

01
Browse and order from the marketplace
Staff place orders on any device. Real-time pricing from 5,000+ verified suppliers.
02
Approve in seconds, not days
Automated approval workflows route POs to the right person based on value and category.
03
Receive goods and confirm delivery
Mobile receiving confirms delivery against the original order. Discrepancies flagged instantly.
04
AI matches and syncs the invoice
3-way match against PO and delivery note. Auto-coded and posted to your accounting system.
